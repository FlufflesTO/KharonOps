Kharon Privacy Notice

Last updated: 11 April 2026

This interim notice applies to the Kharon public site and portal currently served on the tequit.co.za domain for development, pilot, and controlled production testing.

Information We Process

  • identity information used for sign-in, including name and email address
  • operational data required to manage service jobs, scheduling, reports, and closeout evidence
  • technical data such as request logs, security events, and session metadata

Why We Process It

  • to authenticate authorised users
  • to manage field operations, client communication, and service documentation
  • to maintain audit trails, security controls, and platform reliability

Service Providers

The current platform uses Google and Cloudflare services as operational infrastructure. Additional processing may occur through authorised Kharon systems used for scheduling, document generation, and communication rails.

Access Requests

For privacy or access requests, contact connor@kharon.co.za.
